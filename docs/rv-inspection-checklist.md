## RED FLAGS ⚠️ **DEALBREAKERS**

**If ANY of these found, walk away or demand major discount:**

1. ☐ **Multiple roof leaks** - Most common/serious issue
2. ☐ **Sawdust under sinks** - Active wood rot
3. ☐ **Black tank valve binds** - $500+ repair
4. ☐ **Water heater won't heat** - Wiring/control board
5. ☐ **Wrong plumbing fittings** - Factory quality control fail
6. ☐ **Severe LED flickering everywhere** - Electrical system
7. ☐ **Broken plastic everywhere** - Window cranks, knobs, valves
8. ☐ **Multiple appliance failures** - Systemic issues
9. ☐ **Improperly routed propane hoses** - Safety hazard
